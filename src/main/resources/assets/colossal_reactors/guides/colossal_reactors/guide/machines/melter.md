# Melter

<BlockImage id="melter" scale="4" />

The **Melter** turns solid inputs into molten fluid output for your reactor chain or other machines.

## Controls

- **Input slot**: place processable items here (see JEI for valid recipes).

- **Progress bar**: shows current recipe progress; **click it in JEI** (when installed) to open matching recipes.

- **Fluid tank**: hover for fluid type and amount. **D** below the tank dumps stored fluid.

- **Redstone gear button**: cycles redstone behavior (ignore, low, high, etc.—labels update on the button). Hover for the active mode name.
