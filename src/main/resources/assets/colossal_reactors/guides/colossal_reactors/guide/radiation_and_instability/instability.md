# Reactor instability

Some packs enable an extra **instability / pressure** layer on top of ordinary reactor operation. When it is active for your save, the **Reactor Controller** HUD and readouts surface stability-related warnings or limits—follow those prompts and adjust fuel, coolant, or layout as the UI indicates.

Heat sinks and coolant definitions include multipliers that affect that layer only while instability is turned on in config.

If your controller never mentions instability, you can ignore this topic.
