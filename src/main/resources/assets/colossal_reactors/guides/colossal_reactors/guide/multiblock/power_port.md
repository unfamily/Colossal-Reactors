# Power Port

<BlockImage id="power_port" scale="4" />

The **Power Port** is a **face-only** attachment: it forwards generated energy from the reactor to cables, ducts, or machines on the **outside**. There is **no screen**—you wire things up and read energy flow from your network or connected machines.

## Funzionalità

- Place on an outer face of the casing; the **inside** must face the reactor interior.

- Connect compatible energy handlers from other mods on the **outside** of the port.
